Welcome to NoteCollab!

Why Learn Apart, When We Can Learn Together?

Students work best in group projects that are well structured for student success. NoteCollab provides an environment for professors to build a course around student group learning

Learn More

Understand Student Needs

According to a study published by the College Learning Association, after two years 46% of students and after four years 34% of college students have had no significant learning experiences.

Tackle Student Learning

Lectures are impersonal and passive, student group projects are unstructured and often are accomplished by 1-2 students in the group, and common online learning tools are written as references for industry experts, and not for learning

Work Towards Student Outcomes

NoteCollab provides a framework for professors to structure group activities around quantifiable student outcomes and provides analytics to the professor based on successes and failures.
